About Arkitor

Canadian-owned and operated, delivering innovative magnesium oxide building solutions across North America.

Our Story

Engineering Excellence Since Day One

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the building materials industry, Arkitor Building Technologies has become a leading manufacturer of premium magnesium oxide panels in North America.

Our commitment to innovation, quality and sustainability drives everything we do. We've developed proprietary MgO formulations that outperform traditional building materials while maintaining environmental responsibility.

Today, our products are trusted by builders, architects and contractors across residential, commercial and institutional projects throughout Canada and the United States.
