Choose the Right Panel for Your Project

Each Arkitor product is purpose‑built with a specific set of benefits to suit your build—whether exterior cladding, interior partitions or structural floors.

ArkiShield™

Premium magnesium oxide exterior cladding panels engineered for maximum fire resistance, moisture protection and architectural versatility.

  • Class A fire rating with non‑combustible core
  • Waterproof and freeze–thaw resistant
  • High impact and flexural strength
  • Available in textured, smooth or shiplap finishes
Learn More

ArkiCore™

Versatile interior MgO panels for walls, ceilings and partitions. Ideal for high‑moisture environments such as bathrooms, kitchens and basements.

  • Zero VOCs and silica‑free for a healthier indoor environment
  • 100 % waterproof core—will not swell, rot or delaminate
  • High dimensional stability and screw holding strength
  • Available in multiple thicknesses and smooth finish
Learn More

ArkiFloor™

High‑density subfloor and underlayment panels designed for superior load‑bearing capacity and dimensional stability.

  • Strong, smooth base for tile, hardwood and resilient flooring
  • Moisture and mold resistant for bathrooms and basements
  • Lightweight and easy to cut and install
  • Available in square and shiplap edge profiles
