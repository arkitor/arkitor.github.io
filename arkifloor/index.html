Applications

Ideal Uses

  • Subfloor and underlayment for tile, hardwood and vinyl flooring
  • Structural base for modular and prefabricated buildings
  • Basements, bathrooms and other wet‑area floor systems
  • Over concrete slabs to prevent cold and moisture transfer
